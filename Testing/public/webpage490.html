Group Capstone Project

Steam API Backlog Game Recommender

The Gaming Backlog Recommendation Generator helps users decide what to play next based on their Steam library, play history, and personal preferences

Users log in with their Steam account, fill out a brief survey, and receive a ranked game recommendation list

The app provides direct links to game pages and allows users to refresh their recommendations
